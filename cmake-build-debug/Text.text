The odd superstitions touched upon were all prevalent among children
and slaves in the West at the period of this story - that is to say,
thirty or forty years ago.

Chapter 1

Oh ye who tread the Narrow Way
By Tophet-flare to Judgment Day,
Be gentle when the heathen pray
To Buddha at Kamakura!

 He sat, in defiance of municipal orders, astride the gun Zam-Zammah 
on her brick platform opposite [the old Ajaib-Gher — ]the Wonder House, 
as the natives call the Lahore Museum. 
 Who hold Zam-Zammah, that "fire-breathing dragon," hold the Punjab,
for the great green-bronze piece is always first of the conqueror’s loot.
